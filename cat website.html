All about cats

Basic Info on Cats

Cats, also known as Felius Catus, are small animals who are carnivorous.
Normally, they are kept as pets and with the right care, live long lives. Most cats are domestic shorthairs, or called tabby cats.
Cats communicate with meows, purrs, hisses, growls, and trills. Depending on the cat's personality, they love toys and will need stimuli daily

Cool Facts about cats!

  1. Millions of cats are owned in America.
  2. Cats can live for 20 years or more.
  3. Some cats can weigh up to 25 pounds!
  4. Cats give themselves baths 3 times a day!
  5. There are over 71 breeds of domestic cats!
